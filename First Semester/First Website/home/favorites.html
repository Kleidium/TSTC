Favorite TV Shows

Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter is an anime based on the manga of the same name. Two versions of the anime were released and ran from 1999 to 2001 and 2011 to 2014. The show follows Gon Freecss on his journey to meet his father. He learns that his father is a "hunter", which are a class of people that are called to handle paranormal threats to society; he decides to follow in his father's footsteps to find him.

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American crime drama which ran from 2008 to 2013. Walter White is a chemistry teacher who discovers he has cancer. He decides to secure his family's financial future after his death by selling meth with a former student, Jesse Pinkman. Along the way he learns more and more about the criminal underworld and both he and Jesse are changed because of it.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is an anime series with multiple story arcs, and is based on the manga of the same name. There have been many iterations of this anime between 1993 and now. The current arc of the anime revolves around Jolene Kujo, the daughter of the protagonist from the 3rd story arc. In the show, some humans are capable of summoning a manifestation of their willpower called a "Stand", which has supernatural abilities. Jolene plans to escape prison using this power.

Archer

Archer is an adult animated sitcom which began airing in 2009 and is still airing now in 2022. It follows an intelligence agency with Sterling Archer as the main character. Hilarious show that is a parody of "secret agents" and cold war/political humor. Some seasons are workplace sitcoms, and others are self-contained anthologies.

Black Mirror

Black Mirror is a British anthology series which began airing in 2011, and is still airing now in 2022. This series is based on The Twilight Zone and uses technology to comment on social issues. Many episodes contain interesting moral questions and "what if" scenarios based on potential near-future technologies. The show explores how society may change for the worse as technology improves.
