~Sweet, Sweet Hobbies~

Six hobbies of mine are video games, programming, music production, reading books, theater, and travel. I have always loved video games since I was a kid. Programming is a fairly new hobby of mine; I started with modding games, and I'm still learning. I got into making music in high school, and occasionally still dabble in making electronic music. As far as books go, I have always been into reading; my favorite book series is the Dune series by Frank Herbert. Theater is special to me because it helped me come out of my shell and define my personality. Travelling is a favored pastime of mine as well; I feel restless staying in one place for too long...
