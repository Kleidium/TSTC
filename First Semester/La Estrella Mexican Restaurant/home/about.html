About Us

Our story began in 1960, in the city of Valle Hermoso, Tamaulipas. Our founder, Marcello Lopez, dreamed of owning a restaurant. While he lacked the means to open a business, he wasn't about to let that stop him. Marcello spent years perfecting his cooking technique in preparation for his restaurant. In 1966, he began to build popularity from selling fajita tacos on the street made with his own secret sauce. However, it wouldn't be until 1970 that he gathered enough funds to move his dream to America. Fueled by his dedication to his dream and to the American ideal, he managed to garner the same following he did in Mexico. Finally, in 1972, he opened the first ¡La Estrella! location in Dallas, Texas. Ever since then, we have worked hard to provide quality, star-studded Mexican cuisine to the people of Texas. The rest, as they say, is history.
